2. Data Protection Officer (DPO)

The clinic owner or designated administrator serves as the Data Protection Officer responsible for ensuring compliance with RA 10173. For all data privacy concerns, contact the clinic directly via the phone number or Facebook page above.

3. What Personal Data We Collect

When you book an appointment, we collect the following Sensitive Personal Information (SPI) as defined under RA 10173:

  • Full name
  • Mobile phone number
  • Appointment date and time
  • Chief complaint / reason for visit (health information)
  • Insurance or HMO provider (optional)
  • IP address and browser type (for consent audit trail)

5. How We Use Your Data

  • Schedule and confirm your eye clinic appointment
  • Notify clinic staff of upcoming appointments
  • Maintain appointment history for follow-up care
  • Comply with NPC and DOH record-keeping requirements

Your data will not be used for marketing, sold to third parties, or shared outside the clinic without your explicit consent or a lawful order.

6. Data Retention

Appointment records are retained for 10 years from the date of booking, in line with Department of Health (DOH) guidelines for medical records. After this period, records are permanently deleted from all systems.

7. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored exclusively in Cloudflare D1 (SQLite database hosted in Cloudflare's infrastructure). No data is stored on third-party SaaS platforms or shared with analytics services. Access to patient data is restricted to authorized clinic staff authenticated via session tokens. All admin actions on patient records are logged in an audit trail.

8. Your Rights as a Data Subject

Under RA 10173, you have the following rights:

  • Right to be informed — you are reading this policy
  • Right to access — request a copy of your data by contacting the clinic
  • Right to correct — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your data (subject to DOH retention rules)
  • Right to object — object to processing of your personal data
  • Right to file a complaint — contact the National Privacy Commission at privacy.gov.ph

To exercise any of these rights, contact the clinic directly at +63 963 862 9414.

9. Data Breach Response

In the event of a personal data breach, the clinic will notify the National Privacy Commission (NPC) within 72 hours of discovery, as required by NPC Circular 16-03. Affected data subjects will be notified without undue delay when the breach poses a real risk of serious harm.

10. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated to reflect changes in law or clinic operations. The effective date at the top of this page indicates when the latest version took effect.
